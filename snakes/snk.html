OUR SERVICES

diverse species

There are over 3,000 species of snakes found worldwide, except in Antarctica. They can be found in a wide range of habitats, from deserts and forests to grasslands and water bodies.

venomous or non-venomous

Not all snakes are venomous. Only about 600 species are venomous, and even among those, many are not harmful to humans. Additionally, certain species might have specific housing and care requirements that potential owners must meet

legal for your area

If you are interested in keeping a snake as a pet, I recommend contacting local animal control, wildlife authorities, or reptile societies in your region to get accurate and up-to-date information on the legality and regulations surrounding snake ownership.

longest or shortest size

Snakes: The longest snake in the world is the reticulated python, which can reach lengths of over 30 feet (9 meters). The shortest snake is the Barbados threadsnake, which measures about 4 inches (10 centimeters) long.
